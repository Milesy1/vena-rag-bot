How-To: Search for Members,
Attributes, Aliases and GL Codes
With the Modeler Search
Why use this feature?
With the release of the  search featur e for the Modeler, your search results will include easy-to-
navigate contextual hierarchy information when you use the search function. In addition, search
results will be highlighted to help you find the information you need.
In this article, you’ll learn how about the search functionality in the Modeler. 
 
Before you begin
To follow the instructions in this article, you will need at least  Modeler  access.
 
How to
1. Log in to vena.io and select the  Modeler  tab.
